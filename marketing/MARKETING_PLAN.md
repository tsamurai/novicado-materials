## One-Month Marketing Strategy

## Monthly Goals

1. **300 unique visitors** to the public curriculum landing page
2. **40 new student sign-ups** (Google auth completions)
3. **500 impressions** across Twitter and Reddit posts
4. **8 social posts** published (Twitter threads + Reddit posts)
5. **2 public GitHub template repos** published with READMEs

## Content Themes

1. **"Why I built my AI school with zero backend"** — Authentic build-in-public story resonates with developers tired of over-engineered tutorials.
2. **"The 6 tools every AI builder needs in 2025"** — Curated tool list (Zed, DeepSeek, fly.io, Supabase) appeals to beginners overwhelmed by options.
3. **"From tutorial hell to deployed project in 6 weeks"** — Directly addresses the pain point of getting stuck with YouTube tutorials.
4. **"How to deploy a full-stack app for free"** — fly.io free tier + Supabase demo proves the school teaches practical, zero-cost skills.
5. **"My AI project architecture template (free download)"** — Lead magnet that drives sign-ups and positions the school as generous with resources.
6. **"What I learned building a real product with AI agents"** — Showcases Lesson 4 material (AI agents) as cutting-edge and actionable.
7. **"Student showcase: 3 projects built in 6 weeks"** — Social proof that the curriculum produces real results, encouraging enrollment.

## Publishing Calendar

- **Articles:** 1 blog post — "Why I Built Novicado Materials with No Backend"

### Week 4: Recap & Cohort Push
- **Articles:** 1 blog post — "Everything I Learned Marketing an AI School for One Month"
