- 2-3 actionable items:
  1. **Add a sitemap.xml**: Generate and submit a sitemap listing all lesson pages (if they exist as separate routes) to Google Search Console for faster indexing.
  2. **Set a canonical URL**: Ensure `https://novicado-materials.fly.dev` has a self-referencing canonical tag to avoid duplicate content issues (e.g., with `www` or trailing slash variants).
